About R&C Hardware Solutions

East London's trusted source for high-quality refurbished laptops at affordable prices

Our Story

Founded in 2023 by Raz and Cia, R&C Hardware Solutions began with a simple mission: to provide East London residents with access to high-performance, affordable computing solutions while reducing electronic waste.

As technology enthusiasts and entrepreneurs, we recognized the growing need for reliable yet budget-friendly laptops among students, small businesses, and professionals in our community.

What started as a small operation refurbishing a handful of laptops has grown into East London's premier destination for quality refurbished HP, Dell, and Lenovo laptops, with plans to expand our reach across South Africa.

Our Mission & Values

Guiding principles that drive everything we do

Affordability

We believe everyone deserves access to quality technology without breaking the bank. Our laptops offer premium performance at up to 70% off retail prices.

Quality

Every device undergoes rigorous testing and upgrades to ensure it meets our high standards. We only sell laptops we'd be proud to use ourselves.

Sustainability

By extending the life of quality electronics, we reduce e-waste and the environmental impact of manufacturing new devices.

Our Refurbishment Process

Every laptop undergoes a comprehensive 25-point inspection and upgrade process

1

Sourcing

We carefully select business-grade laptops from trusted suppliers, focusing on HP, Dell, and Lenovo models known for their durability and performance.

2

Initial Inspection

Each device undergoes a thorough evaluation of hardware components, cosmetic condition, and functionality to determine necessary repairs and upgrades.

3

Hardware Upgrades

We replace HDDs with SSDs, upgrade RAM when needed, replace worn batteries, and ensure all components meet our performance standards.

4

Software Installation

A fresh installation of Windows 11 Pro is performed, along with all necessary drivers and security updates for optimal performance.

5

Quality Assurance

Each laptop undergoes 24+ hours of stress testing to ensure reliability before being cleaned, packaged, and prepared for sale.
